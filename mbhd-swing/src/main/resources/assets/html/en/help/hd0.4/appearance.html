Appearance

From the Preferences screen select "Appearance"

Change theme

To change the colour scheme of the application simply use the drop down list and click Apply. The change will be immediate.

In general the Dark Theme is used for low light conditions and the Bootstrap theme for higher contrast. The Lite Theme works well in office lighting conditions.

Show balance

When using MultiBit in a public place or other situations where you may be observed it can often be desirable to hide the large font balance information. To switch between them just select from the drop down list and click Apply. The change will be immediate.

Show news alerts

Every so often we will provide an article detailing some aspect of Bitcoin relevant to MultiBit users. This will now trigger an optional alert on wallet unlock with a non-identifying link to the article. Select Yes or No from the drop down list and click Apply. The change will be immediate.
