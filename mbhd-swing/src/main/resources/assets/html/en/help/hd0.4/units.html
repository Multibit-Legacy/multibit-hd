Display

Would you prefer to see a payment described as 160,000 sat or as 0.0016 XBT? To choose what units and number format is used for past and future payments, select "Preferences" and then select "Display".

  1. Choose from the drop down lists which currency symbols you want to use (both Bitcoin and national currency), and what convention you want to use for formatting decimals.
  2. Click "Apply" to change the format to your new preference or click "Cancel" to leave things as they are.

The currency symbols available are determined by your choice of currency in the Exchange settings screen.
